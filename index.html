Shreemat Industries

Trusted Partner in Fabrication & Engineering Excellence

Established by Mr. Vikas Pandey (Mechanical Engineer), Shreemat Industries delivers advanced fabrication services rooted in engineering precision and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Why Choose Us?
We combine decades of practical expertise with a relentless focus on innovation, efficiency, and customer satisfaction. Our team specializes in high-performance fabrication for civil infrastructure, public projects, railways, and industrial construction.

Vision: To be recognized as India’s top supplier of precision-engineered components, setting benchmark standards for quality and reliability.
Mission: Ensure every product delivered is defect-free, on schedule, and crafted with sustainable, modern practices. We invest in our people’s skills and operate with integrity, safety, and environmental responsibility.

From raw material to finished assembly, every step is supported by rigorous inspection, modern machinery, and a culture of continuous improvement.
